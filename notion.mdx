Connect Notion for live access to your pages and databases. Changes in Notion are immediately available—no syncing required.

## Connect

1. Click **Connect Notion**
2. Select your workspace
3. Choose pages/databases to share
4. Click **Allow access**

## What Your Agent Can Do

- Search across shared pages
- Read page content and database entries
- Use content to answer questions

## Managing Connection

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| View status | Green = connected |
| Open Notion | Click **Open Notion** |
| Disconnect | Click **Disconnect** |

## Configuring Tools

After connecting, go to **Actions** → **Notion** to toggle specific capabilities.

## Change Shared Pages

1. Disconnect current connection
2. Reconnect and select different pages

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Connection failed | Enable popups, try again |
| Content not found | Check pages were shared during auth |
| Need different pages | Disconnect and reconnect |

## How It Works

1. **Configure your agent** — Set up your agent's instructions in the Sandbox
2. **Add knowledge** — Upload documents, crawl websites, or connect Notion
3. **Enable integrations** — Connect your APIs for dynamic capabilities
4. **Deploy** — Add the widget to your website
5. **Monitor** — Track conversations and improve over time
